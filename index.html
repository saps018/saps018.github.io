Hello! I'm Saptarshi

'Researcher by profession, artist at heart' thats what I like to call my self. Science and art are not so different than each other, rather they are associated closely. While science seeks explanation for natural phenomena, art provides a subjective interpretation to it. By training, I am a molecular biologist who loves to uncover the mystries our life holds. I am currently working as a postdoctoral fellow at Prof. Bruno Antonny's lab in IPMC-CNRS, France. Besides being a researcher, I am an avid reader. In my leisure times, I write poetry and short stories in my mothertongue bengali. I am also passionate about photography, painting, playing guitar, and traveling.
